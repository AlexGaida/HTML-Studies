Nikita Khurushev

Here is a time line of Mr. Khrushchev's life:

  • 1917- Was elected to the worker's council in Rutchenkovo. Becoming a chairman in the end.

  • 1918- Joined the Bolsheviks (Far-right Marxist faction)

  • 1919- Mobilised into the Red Army as a political commissar (political indoctrination of the miliatry unit)

  • 1921- Became a commissar to a labour brigade in Donbas (at the end of the civil war)

  • 1922- Refused a directorship of Pastukhob mine, preferred the Tekhnikum in Yuzovka.

  • 1925- Elected into the 14th Congress of the USSR Communist Party.

  • 1935- Completed the Moscow Metro, receiving the Order of Lenin for its construction.

  • 1960- Improved relations with the U.S and reduced the Soviet Armed Forces to divert economic resources into Advanced Weaponry.

  • 1961- Oversaw the handling of the shooting down of U-2 Spy aircraft over the USSR soil.

  • 1954 - 1959- Commenced the greatest transfer of technology to the newly appointed Communist party by Mao Zedong.
